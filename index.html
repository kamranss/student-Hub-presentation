      Project Charter

      Project Title

      StudentHUB – Unified Alumni–Student Engagement, Matching & Analytics Platform

      Purpose / Background

      Universities struggle with fragmented alumni data and no unified way to track outcomes. After graduation, engagement drops because systems are scattered and lack value-driven features. StudentHUB is designed as a lifetime digital companion to unify jobs, mentorship, events, news, skills, analytics, and AI recommendations before and after graduation.

      Problem Statement

      GHU lacks accurate, updated alumni career data and a unified system to match students/alumni with jobs, mentorship, or personalized opportunities. Engagement disappears after graduation due to scattered systems and missing value.

      Objectives

      • Centralize profiles, skills, events, news, jobs, and mentorship.
      • AI-powered matching for jobs/mentors/donors using skills and engagement signals.
      • Role-based portals: Students, Employers, University Staff.
      • Admin dashboards for engagement, system health, recommendations.
      • Keep alumni active post-graduation (LinkedIn-like continuity).
      • Scalable architecture for predictive analytics.

      Scope (In)

      • Student dashboard: jobs, events, news, skills, applications, engagement.
      • Employer workspace: postings, candidate analytics.
      • Admin portal: reporting, matching engine, recommendations.
      • AI quick tools: fit check, profile tips, interview questions, cover note.
      • Mentorship discovery + matching.
      • Personalized events & news.
      • Synthetic dataset (2000+ records).
      • Matching engine (jobs, mentorship, donation); engagement score + insights.

      Scope (Out, MVP)

      • Full mobile app.
      • Real-time messaging & chat.
      • Canvas/Blackboard assignment integration.
      • External API connectors (LinkedIn, Indeed, CRM sync).
      • Blockchain credential verification.

      Key Deliverables

      • React-based prototype (Student, Employer, Admin).
      • Synthetic dataset (JSON/CSV) for dashboards and matching.
      • ERD + Data Model + Architecture diagrams.
      • Admin dashboards: Engagement, Recommendations, System Summary.
      • AI-powered quick analysis tools.
      • Project website (GitHub Pages) + final academic paper (IEEE-style).

      Success Criteria

      • 95% of profiles reach 80%+ completeness.
      • 80% accuracy in AI job/mentor matching.
      • Dashboards show measurable engagement trends.
      • Role-based access works end-to-end.
      • Insights support solution goals (events, jobs, skills).
      • Students continue usage beyond academic period.

      Risks & Constraints

      • Alumni adoption may be slow without clear value.
      • Synthetic data may not mirror real patterns.
      • Time limits full integration (mobile, CRM connectors).
      • AI accuracy depends on data quality; privacy/bias management needed.

      Stakeholders & Roles

      Stakeholder Role / Responsibility
      Students / AlumniUse platform for jobs, events, mentoring, skill tracking.
      EmployersPost jobs, review candidates, analyze engagement.
      University StaffManage events, news, dashboards, matching, analytics.
      Instructors & ProfessorsSupport mentorship matching, identify donors.
      Admin (System Owner)Manages permissions, reporting, architecture controls.
