7. Ethical & Social Considerations

Privacy & AI Usage

  • AI never receives personal identifiers; it only uses skills, experience, education, interests, and preferences.
  • Users choose what to share and can delete or export their data permanently (FERPA/GDPR aligned).

Accessibility & Inclusion

  • Semantic HTML, contrast, and keyboard navigation are supported across the layout.
  • Match scores exclude sensitive attributes to reduce algorithmic bias.
  • Voice navigation is planned for future versions.
